OFFICE OF DEAN EXAMINATION NOTICE Special Re-appear Examination, July August 2024 This information is for the all eligible students (passed out batches and passing out batches 2025) having backlogs in Even semester, that the University is conducting re-appear examinations in the month of July- August 2024. The students having backlogs can apply for the re-appear examinations. The option for filling the re-appear form will be enabled on Chalkpad w. e. f. July 12th, 2024. Students are advised to check the Chalkpad regularly for datesheets and other updates. Mode of Examination: The re-appear examination of a student for a particular course will be held in the same mode as it was conducted during his /her regular semester examination. For example if the mode of examination for a course during the regular semester exam was offline then the re-appear examination will also be held in offline mode and vice-versa. All online re-appear examinations will be conducted in computer lab in campus. Procedures for applying for re-appear examination Chalkpad 1. Login to your chalkpad account 2. 3. Select the courses (offered courses only) in which you want to appear. 4. Click online payment. 5. Make payment and keep your fees paid receipt for reference (Check that you have enabled pop-up). Note: After payment if receipt is not generated wait for 24 Hrs and if amount is not credited into your account then click online fee receipt complaint. (The above procedures are mandatory for applying for re-appear examinations, without the above procedures your ID and name will not get registered for re-appear examinations.) Other important instructions . Last date for submission of application form along with fee is 22nd July, 2024; thereafter no form will be accepted. Under Payment Link - Select Apply for Reappear.
